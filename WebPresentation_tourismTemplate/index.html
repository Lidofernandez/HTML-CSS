Come and enjoy the most famous Inca fortress

Machu Picchu, Peru

Machu Picchu is tangible evidence of the urban Inca Empire at the peak of its power and achievement; a citadel of cut stone fit together without mortar so tightly that its cracks still can not be penetrated by a knife blade.

Check out all the Wildlife

Due to its incredible plant life, Machu Picchu teems with wildlife, and its greatest ambassadors are its birds, such as lovely hummingbirds, the Andean cock of the rock, and papagayos.

Twenty thousand years of civilization

Evidence of settlement in Peru dates back thousands of years but, except for some scattered ruins, little is known of these early peoples. Once, you come to Peru do not forget to visit the most important ruins and civilizations:

  • The Inca empire
  • Floating Islands of Lake Titicaca
  • The sun and moon pyramids

Peruvian Gastronomy

Peruvian cuisine, considered among the best in the world, inherited its innovation, mix and flavors developed over a long process of cultural exchange between the Spanish, Africans, Chinese, Japanese among others. The most popular are:

  1. Ceviche
  2. Cui
  3. Lomo saltado
  4. Aji de gallina
